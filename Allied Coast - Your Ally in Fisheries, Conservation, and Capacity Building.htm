Our Story

Founded by James LaFlamme, Allied Coast emerged from over 15 years of experience working closely with First Nations communities on Vancouver Island's west coast.

Having grown up in a First Nations community with a stepfather who is Indigenous, James developed a deep understanding of both traditional knowledge and modern business practices.

Allied Coast was built on the principle that business development and environmental stewardship can work hand-in-hand when approached with respect for Indigenous knowledge and values.
